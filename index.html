Youtube Tip


You can download any Youtube video in the browser by adding 'ss' before youtube in the link and select the desired resolution and go for it.

Phone Tip


Never clear the recent apps in your phone. This will slow down your phone by a small amount as killing the recent apps will require more battery when you try to open the app again!
PS:External apps like cleanMaster will do the same thing to provide you additional RAM but it does drain your battery, so avoid them!

Chrome Tip


Ctrl+T opens a new tab and Ctrl+W closes corresponding tab in Chrome and finally;
Ctrl+Shift+T reopens an accidentally closed tab in Chrome!

Computer Tip


If your pc is hanged or stopped, you can directly open Task manager by pressing CTRL+SHIFT+ESC keys together!
